Wi-Fi Setup Checklist

Press Windows + R
Type "Control Panel"
Open Network and Internet
Open Network and Sharing Center
Click Wi-Fi under "Connections" (see Exhibit 1.0)
Click Properties
Ensure Client for Microsoft Networks is selected
Click Configure
Go to Advanced tab
Set 802.11a/b/g Wireless Mode to best available (see Exhibit 2.0)
Click OK (Wi-Fi may disconnect briefly)
